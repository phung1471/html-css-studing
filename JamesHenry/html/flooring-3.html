Total Flooring Solutions

James Henry are experts in designing, installing and finishing solid wooden floors. We are experienced with all wooden flooring types and can provide floors in Recycled T&G, New T&G, Parquet and Engineered timbers.

Our experts can guide you from the outset to ensure you get the right floor and finish. We are happy to call on site and will work with your designers, architects and builders to develop your ideas. We know what works and what doesn't and can offer great advice when we are involved early in a project.

Our objective is to provide a beautiful classic floor which looks great, adds value, is installed correctly and provides many years of satisfaction.
